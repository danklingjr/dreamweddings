House of Clarendon240 Harrisburg Avenue, Lancaster, PA 17603

The House of Clarendon, situated right off of Harrisburg Pike, is an European Style Cakery, specializing in delicious and attractive style cakes for every occasion. Clarendon hosts a Bridal Cake Gallery where brides may sample and design their own wedding cakes. Our cakes capture the elegance of satin and lace, with the soft glow of romance, whether traditional, contemporary or lovingly funky. So that you enjoy both the look and taste of our creations our fondant is THIN, SOFT and CREAMY you won’t even know they are eating it.
