Submit Your Wedding


Participants in Dream Weddings will receive a copy of their Dream Weddings episode on DVD, and the opportunity to have their wedding showcased on WGAL or the DreamWeddingsPa.com website. Participation does not imply free services by any wedding vendor or venue. Participants must be using a minimum of seven local professionals in the wedding industry.


In order to keep wedding videography consistent throughout the show, Dream Weddings participants will be asked to use LavonFilms for their wedding videography. Regular rates will apply, although participants can expect to receive additional footage shot for the show. Contact LavonFilms.com for rates and more information.
